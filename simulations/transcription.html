Vocabulary

DNA: Deoxyribonucleic acid, the molecule that carries genetic instructions.

RNA: Ribonucleic acid, involved in protein synthesis.

mRNA: Messenger RNA, a copy of DNA used for protein synthesis.

Nucleotide: Building blocks of DNA and RNA (A, T, C, G, U).

Codon: A sequence of three nucleotides that specifies an amino acid.
